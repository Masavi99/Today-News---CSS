Valorant’s Yoru Triggers Global Rift: Mysterious Dimensional Glitches Reported Across Major Cities

Authorities and tech analysts are scrambling after unexplained “rift-like” visual distortions began appearing in several major cities overnight, with eyewitnesses claiming a masked figure resembling Valorant agent Yoru stepping in and out of reality. Security cameras captured flashes of blue light, echoing footsteps, and brief duplications of individuals—sparking online speculation that the duelist known for manipulating dimensions has somehow crossed into the real world. Riot Games has declined to comment, but fans are flooding social media with clips and theories, calling it the most immersive “real-life event” ever. Experts warn the phenomenon could be a viral marketing stunt—or something far stranger.
